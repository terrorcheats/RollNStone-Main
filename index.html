We are a tattoo shop based in High Point North Carolina, I started my career in tattoo design 4 years ago when i discovered I have passion to tell stories using art. I believe every tattoo tells a story and has a different meaning to those that have chosen it. The industry of Tattoo design has grown bigger over the years and tattoo are no longer seen as taboo or unacceptable, that makes me inspired as an artist to give it my all when im working with clients from all over the world.
